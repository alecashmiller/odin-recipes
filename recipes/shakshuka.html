Shakshuka

Description

Shakshuka is a delicious North African dish consisting of eggs poached over a spicy tomato sauce. While traditionally eaten as a breakfast or brunch food, shakshuka can be enjoyed at any time of the day with warm bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Half an onion, diced
  • Two cloves of garlic, minced
  • salt
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1/2 tbsp harissa paste
  • 1/4 tsp cumin
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 1/8 tsp cayenne
  • 14 oz canned whole peeled tomatoes, crushed
  • 3 eggs

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and pinch of salt. Cook for 10 minutes until onion is mostly translucent.
  2. Add garlic, tomato paste, and harissa. Cook, stirring frequently, for 2 minutes until tomato paste has begun to darken.
  3. Add spices and cook for 1 minute
  4. Add canned tomatoes. Turn heat to medium-low and let simmer for 5 minutes, stirring a couple times.
  5. Crack eggs on top of tomato sauce and cover with lid. Cook for 5-10 minutes until eggs are done to your preference.
  6. Serve with warm bread.
