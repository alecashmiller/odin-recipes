Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti with tomato sauce and parmesan cheese is a classic, comforting Italian dish.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Good canned tomato sauce
  • Parmesan cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Heat pot of water on stove until boiling
  2. When boiling, add spaghetti to pot. Cook until al dente and drain in colander
  3. Add tomato sauce to spaghetti. Top with parmesan cheese
