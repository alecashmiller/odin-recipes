Yogurt Bowl

Description

Yogurt bowls are a delicious, healthy way to start your morning. An endless variety of toppings help to keep them interesting. I usually opt for granola, nuts, and a mix of fresh and frozen fruits.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup greek yogurt
  • 1/2 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • 1/3 cup frozen peaches
  • 1/3 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 2 tbsp macadamia nuts

Steps

  1. Allow frozen fruit to thaw in refrigerator overnight
  2. Mix yogurt and protein powder thoroughly until there are no clumps remaining
  3. Add toppings
  4. Enjoy!
